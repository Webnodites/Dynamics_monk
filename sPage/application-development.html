Application Development

Home Services Application Development
  • Overview

At Nex we address challenges found in different verticals of the IT domain. Our objective is to provide a suite of comprehensive IT services and solutions to organizations at minimized costs. We cater to the challenging and growing demands in the area of software application development for small, mid-size and big enterprises.

We custom build application development based on the requirements of our clients, as often the software needed is not found in the market. Whether it is about customization of certain platforms to full-cycle custom application development, our developers have the experience to build and deliver affordable, reliable and robust software solutions.

As a result driven application development Company, we believe in fostering long term partnerships with our clients and we realize this can be achieved through giving shape to the ideas and requirements of the clients. Our main aim is to create custom application development projects that work for you – the client.

Areas of Expertise in Application Development

We offer a range of software solutions that can vary from stand alone applications and integration to custom web development. Some of our leading software application development services include:

  • Enterprise application development
  • Mobile application development
  • Custom database development
  • Web application Development

Benefits of Hiring Us for Custom Application Development

We Understand:

The main crux of getting started on an application is gaining a complete understanding. We take the initiative to understand your organization’s business strategies, goals and objectives. An in-depth discussion is the first step to build a relationship where success in developing a world-class software application development is guaranteed.

Architecture To Match

Discussions would sound like empty promises if we couldn’t provide you the architecture to match your specific project related objectives. We adopt a practical approach when it comes to building a robust architecture, which is undoubtedly the foundation for creating an outstanding application. With a proven track record of having worked on innumerable application development projects, we highlight to our clients the areas that need to be worked upon and prioritized.

Project Manager

In order to solve any kind of issues that might arise during the development of the project, we appoint a project manager on day one to work and coordinate with the client regularly. We are determined to offer complete transparency, reliability and ensure that the application reaches the market right on time.

Development

As a leading application development company, our focus is on providing development that is reliable, scalable and strong. We ensure the application is flexible enough that tomorrow amends can be made to it and can include any new feature or options. With our strong knowledge and expertise in different technologies such as Java, .Net, Python, Android and iOS, we can offer the versatility you may require for building different applications.

Testing and Support

QA testing is an important aspect of custom application development where our QA specialists test each and every component and area of the application before it is launched in the market place. Any bugs found are rectified immediately. Alternately, our strong support team helps our clients to seamlessly integrate the application in their organization. Available 24/7 and 365 days a year, they work sincerely to provide any technical support required.
