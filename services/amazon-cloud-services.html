Amazon Cloud Services

Home Services AWS Cloud Solutions
  • Overview

Get rid of the high maintenance charges that the in-house server demands and replace it with Amazon Web Services (AWS) now!

Developed by Amazon, the AWS is one of the most-used cloud platforms with high computing capacity and various database management facilities designed to help a business grow at low costs. The AWS cloud solutions were developed to make the storage easy and simple for everyone without the huge charges to deploy and maintain the servers separately. NEX offers an array of AWS cloud services and AWS consulting services from India to transform the way you store, access and manage information in your organisation. Our managed AWS services are designed to help our clients take the right approach towards the cloud storage platform and drive business innovation and future planning.

Custom-Made AWS Cloud Solutions

Businesses can concentrate on running the business and keep storing their information securely on the AWS cloud platform. AWS cloud solutions have become an integral part of many organisations’ operations due to the extensive infrastructure and flexibility of services it offers.

Every business has some specific expectations from AWS and to put this cloud platform to its best use, you need to customise the platform according to your needs. We, at NEX, bring to you managed AWS services wherein we make it simple and easier to transition to the AWS cloud platform. Our AWS cloud solutions India are intended to help you to become natural in using this platform.

Rewarding AWS Cloud Services India

If this is the first time you are using a cloud platform or migrating from an in-house server or another cloud to AWS, we’ll assist you in making the process smoother. Our AWS Cloud Services India is designed to offer a no-fuss, easy start to using the AWS platform efficiently from day one with a host of other advantages.

  • Budget-Friendly: The AWS platform is much more cost-effective when compared to the in-house servers. Also, you can cut down on hiring extra workforce, space and save up on the maintenance costs, not to mention the remedies for downtime. Our services for AWS cloud computing India will help you to keep your costs as minimal as possible while getting all the advantages of the platform.
  • Auto-Scaling Features: In the AWS system, you can easily add or remove servers based on the amount of storage space you require with the CloudWatch. Further, we will guide you to keep an eye on your metrics from the CloudWatch and facilitate in analysing and maximising the usage.
  • Complete Flexibility: You can construct any kind of storage space you want within your space in AWS. You can choose the programming language, the databases, applications and so on. We provide AWS cloud services India to create a tailored virtual cloud space for your business for easy usage across the organisation.
  • Highly Secure: As it is developed and maintained by Amazon, one of the world’s biggest tech giants, you don’t need to worry about the security. They maintain a very secure hardware and software infrastructure with built-in firewalls, MFA and private subnets that let you experience the best cloud service through AWS. Further, it also leverages the CloudTrail and Identity and Access Management to keep the line of activities of various users separate.
  • No-Loss Billing Models: Since the AWS is billed per hour, you only need to pay for what you use. You can use the AWS system for hosting a single-page website, have a CDN platform or use it as a data warehouse for as long as you want and once you deactivate your account, your billing stops at that exact hour.

Introducing NEX’s AWS Consulting India

When you are already using an AWS platform, but doubtful if you are using it to its maximum advantage, thinking about cutting down some servers or have any other issues, then you can come to our AWS specialists. We have acquired immense experience as AWS consulting partners.

As a part of our services for AWS consulting India, we guide businesses to adapt to the AWS platform, use the servers smartly at minimum charges and simplify the usual operations. Our AWS specialists have expertise in working in various AWS systems like:

  • AWS Lightsail
  • AWS VPC
  • AWS EC2 Container Service
  • AWS EC2 Container Registry
  • Amazon S3
  • AWS Lambda
  • AWS Elastic Beanstalk

We can also guide you to choose the best one for your business requirements and further help in customising it.

Innovative AWS Cloud Computing India

AWS can be used by organisations of different nature to store and access data and information. You can use it as a data collection center for big data analytics, as an archiving location to store petabytes of information, as simple business management applications or as ledgers to keep track of transfers. NEX aims to help companies with our customised and managed AWS services to quickly deploy and make AWS a part of their everyday functioning.

Apart from the usual operations of storing and accessing information, we also facilitate data analytics and data lakes, serverless computing, integration of machine learning, IoT and many other enterprise applications with the AWS.

With our AWS best-in-class AWS cloud solutions India, you can accelerate your cloud experience with high flexibility, scalability and rock-solid security that the world of IT needs now.
