Top 7 Extension for Magento 2 Store

Magento is the open source ecommerce platform which was developed by Varien Inc. According to some surveys e-commerce websites chooses Magento whenever they thought of having an e-commerce site making it to be the leader in this field by having the maximum share in the market.

With wide acceptance of Magento worldwide it was necessary to revamp it for greater and better use of therefore Magento 2 came into existence in the year 2015.

Major reasons behind the launch of Magento 2 are to make it more user friendly, fluency and help the organisation to become more efficient.

Since its launch many big e-commerce sites have started to develop their sites on Magento 2 platform making it more useful to the user than its predecessor.

Magento extension development has become very easy with the launch of Magento 2. Following are the top extension for magento 2:

  • Store Locator

    This extension of Magento 2 helps in locating the stores. Customers can now see the complete list of your stores with proper addresses and also the products list. All these details are highlighted on the Google Map.

  • This helps in bringing the customer close to the stores as they now know where the stores located are.

  • Product Widgets

    This extension allows the customers to see the bestselling item on the homepage of the e-commerce site. However this extension doesn’t show out of stock products.

  • Order Tracking

    For many users order tracking is the most important part of the purchase. An order can be easily tracked by the customer so that he/she may be informed about the delivery status. This extension wipes out all the problems related to tracking.

  • Brand Extension

    People have different choices. These choices make them attached to a specific brand. This extension helps in making clear differentiation between brands so that the buyer can see his choice of branded items.

  • Blog Extension

    Blogs sometimes helps in breaking the ice between the buyer and the website. As blog contains proper details about the e-commerce site and the products it allows the buyer to visit the website more often.

  • Emails

    This extension takes care about the after sale service. After the order is received with the customer this extension sends follow up emails in order to know the customer experience. This feature enables the e-commerce site to make necessary changes in the delivery systems depending upon the feedback they get form the customers. The emails are also sent for certain reminders about the sale at your site or for some discounts.

  • Fluent Check-out

    It’s an old saying “all well that ends well”. The last thing that the customer wants to face is the problem while checking out. In order to have a hassle free check-out Magento 2 is very useful.

Magento development services is highly required as more and more e-commerce sites are now being open. It is necessary for the site to provide customer with ultimate satisfaction whenever they visit your site as the competition is very stiff.
