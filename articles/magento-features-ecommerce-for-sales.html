Magento commerce vs. Magentoopensource

The first and the foremost matter the enterprises have to know is the difference between the Magento Commerce and MagentoOpensource.

  • The MagentoeCommerce

    Previously, The MagentoeCommerce was released with two versions. It was called the Magento enterprise and the Magento enterprise cloud. These two types of versions were combined, and this led to the Magento commerce. This became the version that is the premium version of the Magentoopensource. It consists of the cloud-hosting, which is part of the subscription fee.

  • The Magentoopensource

    Previously, the Magentoopensource was termed as the Magento community. The Magentoopensource is the free version of the Magento. It can be downloaded, self-hosted, and customized.

The Magentoopensource is provided for download free of charge. This is a platform that is open-sourced and is free to use. The users will be able to extend it and also configure it as they prefer. The users of the opensource have permission to get a variety of ranges of the extensions and themes that have the backing of the growing community.
