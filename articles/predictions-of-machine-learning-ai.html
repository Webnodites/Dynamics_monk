5 Mindboggling Ml And Ai Advancements That Will Stun The World In 2019!

Wondering how amazingly your cab booking app works? Thinking how it matches the free cab to your ride request? Or, thinking how your email inbox smartly filters Spams, and now, even labels important emails? We will tell you how! When it comes to the filtering of emails, there is this very special algorithm that is responsible for such type of prediction. What is this algorithm? It is nothing but a part of Machine Learning! And, when it comes to the cab booking app, it uses the power of ML and Artificial Intelligence to streamline the process of booking cabs!

Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence are surely much than buzzwords now. You can see the use and wide adoption of both these magical technologies almost everywhere now. Be it your work or at home, AI has been transforming the way we all function. Artificial Intelligence has grown beyond just a technology used to develop driverless cars. It is everywhere, in our smartphones in the form of Google Assistance or Siri or at our homes in the form of the home assistance devices. Also, these technologies are widely used across the corporate work as well.

Let’s have a look at the top 5 advancements of AI and ML in 2019:

AI and ML to be used in Politics more often?

In the year 2018 we were fortunate enough to witness some of the most unexpected uses of Artificial Intelligence, which included a fair amount of usage by the governments and even in politics.

However, now, AI is expected to be used by the political parties as well. Artificial Intelligence will undoubtedly play a vital role in running successful campaigns in the political systems. Various political parties are making good use of the algorithms to gain success at their political campaigns. The use of AI and ML is only expected to grow. Now, AI and ML will be used to identify the key voters. In fact, algorithms could also be used to identify the specific sections of the population to target. Also, AI and ML will help political parties to personalize their campaigns for a specific group. We are even expecting the use of AI powered robots in Politics as well.

Amplified use of ML and AI across different domains

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning are already being used in a plenty of industries like agriculture, aeronautics, defense, automotive etc. Now, in the year 2019, we are expending the increase of the use of AI and ML and various field including healthcare. When it comes to healthcare, AI is expected to carefully study health history of a patient, health records, account demographics etc. to make predictions. There are even some solutions expected to be made in 2019 which will indirectly have the potential to save the lives of the patients.

Also, it is believed that AI and ML will be used more widely in the field of stock market as well. There will be solutions that will offer accurate predictions to boost the stock market performance. The solutions will work using the publicly accessible earnings documents as well as the stock price data. In fact, there is already a test done on a solution which could predict a low performing stock, and the accuracy was somewhere near to 62%. But, we are certainly expecting a lot stronger and powerful tools in 2019.

Mobile AR (Augmented Reality)

The introduction of Pokemon Go and the hype certainly stunned the world. Needless to say, it quickly became the most exciting game of the century. However, this was just the beginning of the introduction of AR powered features. Apple is also trying to make AR a lot more common, with the integration of AR based features in the Apple devices. The latest integration of AR by Apple is the company’s latest multiplayer support in ARKit 2.

AR is expected to work a lot better on the mobile device. And, we can surely expect a plenty of more AR based apps or solutions that will stupefy the audience in 2019! As, AR will become a lot more common. Hence, we will see a plenty of exciting AR based games, apps etc. in the near future.

AI to play a major role in 2019 Cricket World Cup

Sports authorities are trying to make the most of Artificial Intelligence to make predictions during the cricket world cup. The analysis will be based on training the models, and all the data gathered will be used to make predictions. A perfect predictive analysis model will be created for the 2019 Cricket World Cup, with this, the experts will be able to churn out fascinating analysis and reports. This type of analysis will not only benefit the viewers, but it is going to benefit the teams and even the sponsors in many ways as well. Statistical analysis would surely take a new turn with the integration of AI. However, this is not the first time, AI is being used in sports. Many sports authorities and teams across the world have used the power of AI and Big Data to derive valuable insights from the data.

Rampant use of Artificial Intelligence based assistance

AI assistance have already become an eminent part of our day to day lives. But, we can still expect a lot of growth and development in this field. Though, AI assistance is being evolved a lot, but there is still a lot of roam of innovation. In the year 2019, Artificial Intelligence based assistant might be used for a plenty of more activities. Starting from making online purchases to checking the traffic status, there’s a lot that AI assistance can do. Also, AI assistance will be developed more, and it might be a part of the apps as well.

AI and ML are two of the most revolutionary Offshore software Development technologies. Therefore, we can surely expect a lot from these technologies in the near future. Also, it is very evident that both AI and ML will be a part of many industries and not one!
